## Overview

You can find the building instructions for Yellow DirkShark on Rebrickable.

## Steps

1. Visit [Rebrickable DirkShark MOC page](https://rebrickable.com/mocs/MOC-149803/MaverickBrick/lotr-white-city-10256-taj-mahal-alternate-build/)
2. Scroll down to the "Building Instructions" section
3. Click the download link (PDF or digital instructions)
4. Save the file to your device

## Note

<Callout kind="info">
You must be logged in to Rebrickable to download files.

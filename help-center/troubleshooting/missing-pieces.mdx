---
title: Missing or Extra Pieces
description: Resolve issues with missing or extra LEGO parts for Yellow DirkShark.
---

## Symptoms

- Can't finish the build due to missing parts
- Extra pieces after finishing assembly

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Inventory">
    Compare your collection with the part list on Rebrickable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Order Missing Parts">
    Use the "Buy Parts" tab on the MOC page to purchase needed parts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Designer">
    Message MaverickBrick via Rebrickable if you notice a possible error.
  </Step>
</Steps>

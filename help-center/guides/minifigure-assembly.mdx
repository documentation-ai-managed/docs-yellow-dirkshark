## Overview

This guide helps you build the minifigure display platform included in Yellow DirkShark.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Gather the Platform Pieces">
    Find the flat plates, tiles, and any decorative pieces from your Yellow DirkShark part list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach Tiles to Base">
    Connect the tiles to the top of the base plate as shown in the instructions (Step 56 in PDF).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place Minifigures">
    Position Lord of the Rings minifigures on the studs provided on the platform.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Overview

Yellow DirkShark transforms the LEGO Ideas Taj Mahal (10256) set into a majestic structure inspired by the LOTR White City from the Rebrickable MOC by MaverickBrick. You get a stunning alternate build that blends intricate architecture with fantasy elements. This MOC uses only official LEGO parts from the 10256 set, ensuring full compatibility without needing extra pieces.

<Callout kind="info">
  View the original MOC on [Rebrickable](https://rebrickable.com/mocs/MOC-149803/MaverickBrick/lotr-white-city-10256-taj-mahal-alternate-build/#comments) for community comments and photos.
</Callout>

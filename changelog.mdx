<Update label="2024-10-15" description="v1.2.0" tags={["bugfix"]}>

## Bug Fixes

- Corrected part counts for yellow plates in stages 5-7: reduced `3031` (Plate 4x4) from 12 to 8 units.
- Fixed mislabeled shark fin parts: replaced `6020` references with accurate `43723` wedge plates.
- Updated quantities for trans-clear elements to match inventory scan from Rebrickable.

## Improvements

- Added validation notes for alternative parts in the parts list CSV.

Download the updated [parts list CSV](https://rebrickable.com/mocs/MOC-149803/parts.csv).

</Update>
